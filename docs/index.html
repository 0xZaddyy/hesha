How It Works

Four simple steps to permanent privacy

01

One-Time Verification

Verify your phone number once with a trusted issuer using standard SMS or call verification. This is the only time you'll ever need to share your real number.

  • Choose from multiple trusted issuers
  • Standard SMS/call verification
  • Never need to reverify
02

Generate Proxy Number

A unique proxy number is generated using cryptographic algorithms. It looks like a real phone number but contains no information about your actual number.

  • Deterministic generation
  • Unique to you and the issuer
  • Indistinguishable from real numbers
03

Receive Attestation

The issuer creates a cryptographically signed JWT attestation that proves you own a real phone number without revealing what it is.

  • Digitally signed by issuer
  • Contains binding proof
  • Permanent and portable
04

Share Everywhere

Use your proxy number everywhere you'd normally use your phone number. Services can verify it cryptographically without any API calls.

  • Works with any service
  • No spam or tracking
  • Full control over your privacy

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this like a burner phone number?

No, it's better. Burner numbers are temporary and tied to specific services. Hesha proxy numbers are permanent, cryptographically verifiable, and work everywhere.

Can services still verify I have a real phone?

Yes! That's the beauty of it. Services can cryptographically verify you own a real, verified phone number without ever seeing what that number is.

What if the issuer goes offline?

No problem! Verification works completely offline. Once you have your attestation, services can verify it without contacting anyone.

Is this legal?

Absolutely. You're not hiding from law enforcement or evading regulations. You're simply choosing what personal information to share, which is your right.
