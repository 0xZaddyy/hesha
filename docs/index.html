The Solution

Cryptographic Phone Number Proxies

Hesha introduces proxy phone numbers that are cryptographically tied to your real number without revealing it. Services can verify you own a real phone number, but they never see it.

  • One-time phone verification with trusted issuers
  • Receive permanent, cryptographically signed attestations
  • Share proxy numbers instead of your real number
  • Services verify offline without contacting anyone
